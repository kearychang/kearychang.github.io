His Life Story


  • 356BC - Alexander is born in Macedonia
  • 343BC - Alexander becomes pupil to Aristotle
  • 340BC - Alexander joins his father, Philips's army
  • 338BC - Alexander leads attack against Thebans
  • 336BC - Philip is assassinated, Alexander becomes king
  • 335BC - Alexander subdues rebellion in Greek City states
  • 334BC - Alexander departs for Asia

...


  • 334BC - Alexander's Macedonia army pushes into Persia
  • 332BC - Egypt is conquered
  • 331BC - Babylon is conquered
  • 327BC - Alexander marries Roxane of Iran and they bear a son
  • 326BC - Alexander begins short campaign in India, but returns
  • 323BC - Alexander succumbs to a fever and leaves his empire to 3 commanders
